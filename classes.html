Class Descriptions

Yoga

Yoga focuses on flexibility, controlled breathing, and mindful movement. Classes are designed to improve mobility, reduce stress, and create a stronger connection between the body and mind.

Pilates

Pilates emphasizes core strength, balance, and precise movement. Through controlled exercises, participants develop better posture, stability, and overall body awareness.

Kickboxing

Kickboxing combines cardiovascular conditioning with strength training and basic self defense techniques. Classes are fast paced and empowering, helping participants build confidence, coordination, and endurance.
